# Test cases

- Start game and verify MenuScene renders.
- Press Enter and verify PlayScene renders.
- Press G and verify GameOverScene renders.
- Press Esc and verify the app exits cleanly.
- Start World 1-1 and verify the camera scrolls right as Mario advances.
- Verify Mario collides with ground, bricks, question blocks, pipes, and the final staircase.
- Verify coins disappear and the HUD coin count increments when Mario touches them.
- Verify enemies patrol on solid ground, reverse at pipes/blocks, fall into pits, and can be stomped.
- Verify falling into a pit changes to GameOverScene.
- Verify reaching the flag area near the castle changes to WinScene.
