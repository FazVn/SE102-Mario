# Known bugs

- Level 1-1 now has a playable layout, scrolling camera, basic coins, enemies, pits, and win trigger.
- Question blocks, bricks, pipes, and the flagpole still need their full Mario gameplay behavior: item spawning, coin rewards, break/bump animation, pipe transitions, flagpole slide, scoring, timer, and lives.
- Enemy AI is intentionally simple for now: enemies patrol, reverse on solid collision, fall into pits, and can be stomped, but do not yet support shells, squashed animations, or full damage/invulnerability states.
